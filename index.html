I am a second-year computer science PhD student at UT Austin, and a member of the Theory Group. I am fortunate to be advised by Dana Moshkovitz.
Research interests: computational complexity theory, especially
  • Pseudorandomness
  • Hardness of approximation
  • Coding theory
I earned a bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics from UC Berkeley in May 2019. My senior thesis was on the algebraic structure of the sandpile group of a graph, under Nikhil Srivastava. I also worked on algorithmic fairness and controls theory, as you can see below.
