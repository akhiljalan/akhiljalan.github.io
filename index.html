About Me

I am a fourth-year Computer Science PhD student at UT Austin. My research is on statistics, optimization, multi-agent systems, and their applications to alternative proteins.

I am fortunate to be co-advised by Deepayan Chakrabarti and Purnamrita Sarkar.

I am affiliated with the Institute for Foundations of Machine Learning (IFML), the Institute for Emerging Core Methods in Data Science (EnCORE), and Cultivated Meat Modeling Consortium (CMMC).

Previously, I was a data scientist in San Francisco, CA. I earned a B.A. in Applied Mathematics from UC Berkeley in 2019.
